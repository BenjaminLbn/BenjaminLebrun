About

French neuropsychologist and PhD candidate in psychology. My research explores how people perceive phantom costs in human–robot interaction (HRI), including attributions of negative intentions and risks. I specialise in cognitive and social psychology with a background in neuropsycho‑cognitivo psychology. My career goal is to become a university professor.
